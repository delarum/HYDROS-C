# HYDROS-C
Is a large water body regeneration company which uses tracking devices to keep tabs on any temperature, pH, chemical spills, waste dumps and change in the aquatic biolife of large water bodies.

In this project I'll focus more on the Nairobi River and it's topography in attempts to make it's regenaration more easier through constant monitoring
